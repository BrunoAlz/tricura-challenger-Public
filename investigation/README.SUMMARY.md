# Investigation — Executive Summary

> Public summary of `README.md` for the tricura-challenger evaluation. Full
> content held privately; available to the evaluator on request.

## Document type and purpose

This directory is the candidate's second-layer working artefact set — the
reasoning trail, chronology, probe records, and analytical scaffolding
produced during the engagement. None of it is required by the challenge
brief; it is preserved so a reviewer who wants to audit the *how* behind
the deliverables can do so.

## Contents

Most of the working artefacts in this directory are held privately and not
committed to the public repository, because they document the candidate's
exploration process in spoiler-dense form. Public materials in this
directory are limited to the structural overview, source-data extracts
referenced by the audit memo, and per-artefact public summaries describing
what each privately held document contains.

## Availability

The complete unredacted README, the chronology, the investigation journal,
the probe-script catalog, the deferred-work register, and the analytical
graph artefacts are all held privately under the candidate's `.private/`
workspace (not committed to the public repository) and are available to
the evaluator for review on request.
