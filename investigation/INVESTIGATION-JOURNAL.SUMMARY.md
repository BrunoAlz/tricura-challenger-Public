# Investigation Journal — Executive Summary

> Public summary of `INVESTIGATION-JOURNAL.md` for the tricura-challenger
> evaluation. Full content held privately; available to the evaluator on
> request.

## Document type and purpose

A candidate-internal investigation artefact referenced by the audit memo.
The journal is a chronological log of the QA engagement, written so that a
reviewer can reconstruct the reasoning and discipline behind the audit
without relying solely on the polished memo. It is inherently spoiler-dense
because it walks through how each observation was reached, and is therefore
preserved privately rather than published.

## Structure

The journal is organized as chronological entries that follow a consistent
template: **Source** (what was being examined), **Signal** (the specific
observation that drew attention), **Principle** (the general QA or security
heuristic invoked), **Hypothesis** (the working interpretation), and
**Next step** (the planned validation). This structural skeleton is the
only aspect of the journal reproduced here.

## Availability

The complete unredacted document is held privately under the candidate's
`.private/` workspace (not committed to the public repository) and is
available to the evaluator for review on request.
