# Open Doors — Executive Summary

> Public summary of `OPEN-DOORS.md` for the tricura-challenger evaluation. Full
> content held privately; available to the evaluator, sent by E-MAIL

## Document type and purpose

A candidate-internal investigation artefact that catalogs the audit's deferred
follow-up work — surfaces and probes the candidate identified but elected not
to pursue within the audit window. It exists to keep parallel branches
traceable so that diving deep on one finding does not cause others to be
forgotten.

## Structure

Entries are organized by priority, with each carrying a brief rationale,
prerequisites, expected payoff, and cost notes. Specific contents are held
privately.

## Availability

The complete unredacted document is held privately under the candidate's
`.private/` workspace (not committed to the public repository) and is
available to the evaluator for review on request.
